Thank you for purchasing the Flat Shaded Japan Pack!


You can immediately start with creating your first environment. 
Open the Prefabs folder, take a look at the variety of assets and start constructing your scene.
Demo and Showcase scenes are included aswell.


Also check out the other packs from the 'Flat Shaded' series


Have fun with your project!



Tips:
-Use the Post Processing Stack and switch to Linear Color Space for better visuals (Availabe for free as a Unity Plugin)

-In order to use the asset pack with the Universal Render Pipeline, simply convert Project Materials to URP Materials
